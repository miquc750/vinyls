The Works

Queen

Harry Styles releases his third album, titled 'Harry's house', which is preceded by the wonderful 'As it was'. Two and a half years have passed since the release of his previous studio work and, since then, Styles has participated in two shoots, has toured constantly -stopping tickets wherever he went- and has given him time to develop this new work, but above all, during this time the Briton has been catapulted to global stardom.

Thanks to 'Fine line', the pop world definitively surrendered to one of the greatest talents of our days and the musician from Redditch made history, breaking the sales record for a British artist on Billboard in the first week and becoming the first UK male artist to enter directly at #1 on Billboard with two consecutive albums.
